Open Positions

We currently have several full-time positions at levels of "Senior Researcher", "Research Scientist" and "Research Engineer" available in our group. If you are interested to be referred, please send your resume/CV to my company email address [ziran dot wang at toyota dot com] with an email title "Full-Time Application Full Name".

The detailed information about the full-time positions is:

1. This position is expected to start in Spring 2022.
2. The duty of the position can be jointly defined with the director under the scope of the ongoing projects within the Intelligent Connected Systems Group, which are generally related to intelligent vehicles and cyber-physical systems technology.
3. Peer-reviewed publications, U.S. patent filings, and/or proof-of-concepts are the major deliverables of the position.

The preferred qualifications for the candidates are:

1. Master or Ph.D. degree in computer science, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering or a related major in a full-time, accredited graduate degree program.
2. Research or project experience regarding any of the following research fields, including autonomous driving, intelligent/connected vehicles, machine learning, big data, cyber-physical systems, cloud computing, driver behavior, human factors, ride sharing, AR/VR, and/or game engine.
3. Fluent coding skill with MATLAB, Python and/or C++.
4. A proof of research publication record is a plus.

Perks of working at InfoTech Labs:

1. A research-oriented facility located in the center of Silicon Valley.
2. Competitive wages and paid holidays.
3. Onsite and remote working are both available. Housing and relocation assistance for those that qualify.
4. 1-1 supervised by a research mentor at the principal or senior principal level.
